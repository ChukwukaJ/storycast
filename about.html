Our Mission

Storytelling that leaves no one behind

StoryCast was built on the belief that great stories deserve to reach every listener — regardless of ability, device, or bandwidth.

What We Do

We produce and curate audio and video stories from African creatives and the global diaspora. Our content spans documentary, fiction, interview, and music — all united by a commitment to authentic voice and accessible format.

Every piece of content on StoryCast comes with a full text transcript. Video content includes closed captions. We partner with creators to ensure that the accessibility layer is part of the story — not an afterthought bolted on at the end.

Accessibility Features

📄 Full Transcripts

Every audio episode includes a full text transcript, readable inline or downloadable as a PDF. Transcripts include speaker labels, timestamps, and translations where applicable.

🎬 Closed Captions

All video content includes English closed captions via WebVTT. Captions can be toggled in the native video player. We review captions for accuracy before publishing.

⌨️ Keyboard Navigation

The entire site is navigable by keyboard. Use Tab to move through interactive elements, Enter or Space to activate links and buttons. A "Skip to main content" link appears on focus at the top of every page.

🎨 Colour & Contrast

All text meets or exceeds WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body text). Our gold accent (#E8C547) on near-black (#0D0D0D) achieves a contrast ratio of 10.6:1.

🔇 Reduced Motion

Users who prefer reduced motion (set via OS accessibility settings) will experience no transitions or animations. We respect the prefers-reduced-motion media query across all pages.

How to Engage

StoryCast content is free to access. To get the most from your experience:

  1. Browse stories on the Home page — each card shows format and runtime.
  2. Open any story to access the embedded player, episode notes, and transcript.
  3. Use the Related Stories sidebar to explore similar content.
  4. If you encounter any accessibility issues, contact us directly.
